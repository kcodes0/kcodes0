# Account Issues FAQ

## Account Bans and Suspensions

### My account was banned! What can I do?

If your account has been disabled, you will typically see a message stating "Your account has been disabled after an automatic review of your recent activities." Anthropic uses automated systems to detect violations of their **Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)** and **Terms of Service (ToS)**, and these systems may flag accounts based on usage patterns, content, or behavioral signals. The first step is to carefully review Anthropic's Usage Policy to understand what may have triggered the action. Common reasons for bans include generating content that violates the AUP, using VPNs or proxies that make your traffic appear suspicious, accessing Claude from unsupported regions, bot-like or automated usage patterns on consumer plans, sharing account credentials, or routing third-party tool requests through a consumer Claude account. If you believe the ban was issued in error, you should submit an appeal (see below).

### How do I appeal an account ban?

To appeal a ban, visit the **Anthropic Support Center** at [support.claude.com](https://support.claude.com) and fill out the official appeal form with your account information, so the Safeguards team can investigate. You can also email **support@anthropic.com** directly. When submitting your appeal, include your account email address, a detailed and honest explanation of your use case, and any context that might clarify why the automated system flagged your account. Be transparent about your activities -- making false claims can harm your appeal. Do **not** send multiple follow-up emails, as this will not speed up the process and may be considered harassment. Official processing typically takes **5 to 15 business days**, though complex cases or high-volume periods can extend the timeline to several weeks. Whether your account can be reinstated depends on the severity of the violation -- minor or accidental violations may be reversible, while serious policy violations often result in permanent bans.

### My account is temporarily suspended. How long will it last?

Temporary suspensions are different from permanent bans and are typically issued for less severe or first-time policy violations. The duration varies based on the nature of the violation and is determined by Anthropic's Safeguards team. During a suspension, you will not be able to access Claude or any associated services. You should receive an email or notification explaining the reason for the suspension. If no timeline is provided, you can contact **support@anthropic.com** for clarification. Response times may be extended during periods of high support volume, particularly following major product launches or updates. To avoid future suspensions, carefully review the **Acceptable Use Policy** and ensure your usage patterns do not trigger automated detection systems.

## Login and Access Problems

### I can't log into my account. What should I try?

Start by confirming you are using the correct email address and password combination at [claude.ai](https://claude.ai). Check if you are using the right authentication method -- Claude supports email/password login, Google OAuth, and Apple Sign-In, and you must use the same method you originally signed up with. Clear your browser cache and cookies, or try a different browser or incognito/private window. Ensure you are not using a VPN or proxy, as some IP addresses and regions may be blocked or flagged. If you are on a corporate or school network, firewall rules may be blocking access to Claude's servers. Check [status.anthropic.com](https://status.anthropic.com) to see if there is an ongoing service outage. If none of these steps resolve the issue, contact support at [support.claude.com](https://support.claude.com) with details about the error message you are receiving.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the Claude login page at [claude.ai](https://claude.ai), click the **"Forgot password?"** link. Enter the email address associated with your account, and Anthropic will send a password reset link to that email. The reset link is time-sensitive, so use it promptly after receiving it. Check your spam or junk folder if you do not see the email within a few minutes. If you signed up using Google OAuth or Apple Sign-In, you do not have a Claude-specific password -- use the respective "Continue with Google" or "Continue with Apple" button instead. If you no longer have access to the email address on your account, you will need to contact Anthropic support for manual account recovery assistance.

### I'm getting an "Invalid email or password" error

This error usually means the credentials you entered do not match an existing account. Double-check that you are typing your email exactly as registered, including any periods, underscores, or domain variations (e.g., gmail.com vs. googlemail.com). Verify you are using the correct sign-in method -- if you signed up with Google OAuth, the email/password login will not work because no password was set for that account. Try resetting your password through the "Forgot password?" link to confirm your email is registered. If you recently changed your password, make sure you are using the new one and that your browser's saved password has not auto-filled the old one. Contact support if the issue persists after these steps.

### Claude says I don't have access. What does this mean?

An "access denied" or "you don't have access" message can appear for several reasons. You may be trying to use a feature that requires a paid subscription (such as certain models or Claude Code features) while on the Free plan. If you are on a Team or Enterprise plan, your administrator may not have provisioned access for your account. Some features may be region-restricted or in limited rollout, meaning they are not yet available in your geographic area. If you recently upgraded your plan, try logging out and back in to refresh your session. If you believe you should have access based on your plan, check your account settings to confirm your subscription is active and contact support with the specific error message and the feature you are trying to access.

## Account Recovery

### I can't access my account because I changed email addresses

If you changed your email address outside of Claude's settings (e.g., changed your Google email), you may lose access. Contact Anthropic support at [support.claude.com](https://support.claude.com) with both your **old email address** and **new email address**, along with any identifying information about your account (such as your subscription plan or approximate sign-up date). Anthropic's team will verify your identity before making changes to your account email. This process may take several business days. To prevent this issue in the future, always update your email through Claude's account settings first before making changes to your email provider. If you used Google or Apple sign-in, the email change must be reflected with that identity provider, not directly with Anthropic.

### I deleted my account by accident. Can I recover it?

Account deletion is generally **permanent** once confirmed. When you delete your account, your conversation history, settings, and associated data are removed according to Anthropic's data retention policies. If you recently initiated deletion and believe there may be a grace period, contact **support@anthropic.com** immediately with your account email and explain the situation. There is no guarantee of recovery, but acting quickly may help. If you had an active paid subscription at the time of deletion, the billing is handled according to the terms of your plan -- contact support for specifics about any remaining charges or credits. Creating a new account with the same email address is possible, but none of your previous data or conversations will be restored.

### Someone else is using my account. What should I do?

If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, take immediate action. First, **reset your password** through the login page. If you use Google or Apple sign-in, change your password with that provider and revoke any suspicious sessions. Then contact Anthropic support at [support.claude.com](https://support.claude.com) and report the unauthorized access with any details you have, including suspicious conversations you did not initiate, unexpected usage patterns, or changes to your account settings. Anthropic can investigate and may be able to show you recent login activity. If your account was compromised and used in ways that violated the Usage Policy, explain this in your report so that any resulting flags or bans are reviewed in the proper context. Enable two-factor authentication if available, and avoid reusing passwords from other services.

## Profile and Settings

### How do I delete my account permanently?

To permanently delete your Claude account, go to your **account settings** on [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and look for the account deletion option. You will be asked to confirm the deletion, and the process is typically **irreversible**. Before deleting, download or save any important conversations, as all data including chat history, Projects, uploaded files, and preferences will be permanently removed. If you have an active paid subscription, you should cancel it first to avoid further charges -- account deletion does not automatically stop billing in all cases. After deletion, your email becomes available for creating a new account, but no previous data will carry over. If you are on a Team or Enterprise plan, contact your administrator, as individual account deletion may be managed differently.

### How do I change my email address?

You can update your email address through the **account settings** page on [claude.ai](https://claude.ai). Navigate to your profile settings and look for the email change option. You will need to verify the new email address by clicking a confirmation link sent to it. If you signed up with Google or Apple sign-in, your email is tied to that identity provider and cannot be changed directly within Claude -- you would need to contact support for assistance. Keep in mind that changing your email will affect how you log in going forward. If you encounter issues during the process, contact Anthropic support with your current and desired email addresses for assistance.

### Why can't I access certain features?

Feature access depends on several factors: your **subscription plan** (Free, Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise), your **geographic region**, and whether a feature is in **general availability or limited rollout**. Free plan users have access to basic Claude chat but may not have access to advanced models like Opus, Claude Code, Projects, or certain integrations. Some features are released gradually and may not be available to all users simultaneously. If you recently upgraded your plan, log out and back in to refresh your session and permissions. Check the [Claude changelog](https://claude.ai/changelog) and [Anthropic's blog](https://www.anthropic.com/news) for announcements about new feature availability. If a feature should be available on your plan but is not, contact support with your account details and the specific feature in question.
